The essence of Life Insurance:

Let us understand what the real essence of Life insurance is all about -

Life Insurance is an agreement between the insurance company and the policyholder wherein the insurer guarantees to pay assured amount to the insured at the end of specified time period or at the time of the demise of the insured whichever is sooner. The insured pays a predefined sum of money in the form of premium either at regular intervals or in a lump sum. This is the technical definition.

However, Life insurance really is your Plan B. It is the secure and safe way to protect your family against financial contingencies. Not only does it protect you and your family members but it also provides you with Tax benefits as the premium paid towards Life insurance gets tax exemption under Section 80C.

You must be wondering how much coverage is required. This depends on various factors like marital status, age, dependents, family history, etc. Moreover, your life insurance coverage must be reviewed from time to time as your priorities change according to time.
