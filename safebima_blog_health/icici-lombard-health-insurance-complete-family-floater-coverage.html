ICICI Lombard health coverage family floater plan offers an umbrella of benefits for a pre-decided duration during which you are covered. Comprehensive Coverage Benefits :

Health Insurance are generally of two types – basic plan and comprehensive plan. It is always advisable to buy a comprehensive plan rather than basic plan. As the name suggests, comprehensive plan covers all the basic medical needs for you and your family. The most important benefit of comprehensive is that it covers in-patient treatment and out-patient treatment. That implies, most of the basic medical charges such as medical tests, doctor’s consultation, pharmacy expenses are also covered. Most of these provide coverage for hospital stays without any restrictions. In short, you or your family doesn’t need to worry at all about anything if you are covered under the comprehensive health coverage from ICICI Lombard. Comprehensive can also be customized using a top-up option for seeking any additional benefits that may not be covered or for extreme medical conditions.
